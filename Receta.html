Blueberry Danishes

These quick and easy blueberry danishes are sure to impress with their flaky pastry, creamy filling, and burst of fruity flavor.

"These Blueberry Danishes are pure pastry perfection! Flaky, creamy, and bursting with blueberry goodness. A must-try recipe that's as easy as it is delicious."

Ingredients

    • 1 sheet puff pastry, thawed
    • 1/2 cup blueberry jam or fresh blueberries
    • 1/4 cup cream cheese, softened
    • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1 egg (for egg wash)r
    • Powdered sugar (for dusting)

Instructions

    1. Prep Pastry: Begin by cutting the thawed puff pastry sheet into squares or rectangles. Lay them out on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

    2. Add Filling: In a small bowl, mix the softened cream cheese with a touch of granulated sugar for a creamy sweetness. Spread a dollop of this mixture onto the center of each pastry piece.
    3. Berries or Jam: For a burst of flavor, spoon blueberry jam over the cream cheese. Alternatively, scatter fresh blueberries over the mixture for a juicy, natural sweetness.

    4. Fold and Seal: Gently fold the corners or edges of the pastry squares over the filling. Pinch the edges to seal, creating a neat envelope shape.

    5. Egg Wash: Whisk an egg to create an egg wash, then brush it lightly over the tops of the folded pastries. This will lend them a lovely golden hue as they bake.

    6. Bake to Perfection: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the baking sheet in the oven and bake for around 15-20 minutes. Watch them closely; you're aiming for a beautiful golden brown finish.
